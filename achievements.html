🌟 Wadhwani Foundation IGNITE Program
Innovation Entrepreneurship

I was selected for the Wadhwani Foundation IGNITE Program — a highly competitive innovation and entrepreneurship initiative designed to help students build real startup ventures from the ground up.

Within the program, I serve as the UI/UX Designer and Product Planner for Team RapidReach — a four-person team building a hyperlocal emergency response platform for urban and semi-urban India.

The IGNITE program pushed me to think beyond just coding — to understand users deeply, validate ideas with research, plan product features strategically, and present to mentors and evaluators with confidence.

I contributed to multiple formal milestone deliverables, co-designed the full product experience, and helped steer a major strategic pivot that improved the platform's reach and usability.
